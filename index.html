About Me

As you can see my name is Michael Rodriguez. One of the few crazy Colorado natives that still live here. I recently took a trip to Japan! It was my first time out of the country! Now I want to travel more and also go back to Japan to explore what I missed.

The picture I have here is from my solo trip to Disney Sea in Tokyo, Japan. I didn't know anyone there and did not speak the local language very well but it was such a blast. Everyone was so friendly, the park was very clean and the entire experience was just amazing. I stayed the entire day there and can't wait to go back.

This year I am already set to head out to Spain and Portugal on a 13 day trip in August. There will be a few friends I meet on the Japan trip going so it should be just as awesome. It really is amazing to just get out and explore.

I have been working in the Information Technology field for 12 years now and currently working as a Level III Deskside Technician. With this course I hope to change direction from working with hardware to working with coding and software.
